About us

Medlight for Medical Equipment and Services, is one of the leading specialized medical solutions providers in United Arab Emirates. Our specialty is to provide a full package of services to achieve the desired level of quality and conform to the demands of the healthcare sector. They include FANR e-licensing, DOH inspection preparation, safety assessment, radiation protection training medical radiation technologists, and quality assurance of medical imaging equipment X-ray equipment services like CT scan, CBCT, medical X-ray, and Dental X-ray.
